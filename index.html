About Kleen Code

headed by a Counseling psychologist and a Janitorial manager for one of the top cleaning industries in Nigeria for several years, the creation of KLEEN CODE LIMITED became a timely call as we took out time to study the common wishes of clients within the cleaning industry. Today, we carefully, professionally and flexibly designed packages to meet the taste and budget of individuals and corporate organizations at all level. As a result of our passion for the job and client’s satisfaction, we take that extra steps to tend to the needs of our clients to seal their trust in our capability. The recruiting, counselling and other humanitarian services provided by our co-partner, YESTERWAYS SOLUTION also known as Behavioral Engineers has being our greatest asset. Having a collections of great minds with proven track records of determination and dedication to the satisfaction of our clients remains our common goal. With such an environment where pride and dignity in labour are highly promoted, it gives all team members that sense of belonging which is positively serving as our motivation in contributing our own quarter for our client’s satisfactions and national growth.
